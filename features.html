Features

User Management

  • User Registration: Sign up for a new account. Provide basic info like your username, email, and password to get started.
  • User Log In: Access your account by logging in with your username and password. Gain access to all your information within the system.
  • User Log Out: Securely log out of the system when you're finished using it, especially on shared devices, to protect your account.
  • Update User Profile: Easily modify your profile information such as your name or profile picture to keep it up to date.

Contact Management

Create Contact

Add new contacts to your list with details such as name, phone number, and additional information.

Edit Contact

Modify contact details such as phone numbers or addresses as needed to keep your contacts up to date.

Delete Contact

Remove contacts from your list when they are no longer needed or relevant.

Share Contact

Share contacts with others, pending your approval before they're added to their lists.

Get Approval before Accepting a Contact Shared

Review shared contacts before they are added to your list, ensuring they meet your criteria.

Change Status of Approval

Manage the approval status of shared contacts for better organization and control over your contacts.

Send Message to Saved Contact

Directly message saved contacts from within the app for convenient communication.

Get All Contacts by User ID and Category

Filter contacts by user ID and category for efficient organization and retrieval of information.

Get All Contacts by User ID

View all contacts associated with a specific user ID, simplifying contact management.

Suggest Contact Name

Receive name suggestions based on characters in phone numbers, first names, or last names to streamline contact creation.

Find Contact by Any Character in User Phone Number, First Name, or Last Name

Effortlessly search for contacts using any characters in their phone numbers, first names, or last names for quick access.
